FAQs

Frequently asked questions

How long does a typical project take? +
Most projects take between 4-8 weeks from kickoff to launch, depending on the scope and complexity. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation so you know exactly what to expect.
What platforms do you work with? +
We primarily work with Webflow for its design flexibility and powerful CMS. We also use Spline for 3D elements, Figma for design, and can integrate with various third-party tools and APIs as needed.
Do you offer ongoing support? +
Yes! Every project includes a post-launch support period (30-90 days depending on the package). We also offer monthly retainer plans for ongoing maintenance, content updates, and design improvements.
What's your design process like? +
Our process follows four phases: Discovery (understanding your brand and goals), Design (creating visual concepts), Build (developing the website), and Launch (testing and going live). You're involved at every step.
Can you help with branding and content? +
While our core focus is web design and development, we offer brand strategy guidance as part of our Enterprise package. We also partner with trusted copywriters and brand strategists for comprehensive projects.
